Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Suivi Factures is a personal automation tool used exclusively by its owner. This privacy policy explains how data is handled.

2. Data Collected

This application accesses the following Google services on behalf of its sole user:

  • Google Sheets — to read and write utility bill data
  • Google Drive — to export spreadsheet files

3. How Data is Used

All data accessed is owned by the application owner. No data is shared with third parties. No data is sold or used for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored in the owner's personal Google Sheets and on a private NAS (Network Attached Storage) device.

5. Third Party Services

This tool uses Make.com (Integromat) as an automation platform. Please refer to Make.com's privacy policy for their data handling practices.
